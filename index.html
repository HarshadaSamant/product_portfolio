Who is Harshada Samant?

Harshada Samant is a certified Product Owner and Senior Technical Business Analyst with over 6.5 years of cross-functional experience in healthcare IT, fintech, and product development/management. She blends business insight with technical fluency to drive product strategy, data-driven innovation, and stakeholder alignment in high-impact environments.

Currently at Signify Health (a CVS Health company), Harshada leads internal platform initiatives as a Product Owner for the Data Ingestion Team and Client Relationship Management Team, where she:

Manages agile delivery of ingestion pipelines and data workflows supporting over 4.35 million users,
Enables membership growth and self-scheduling tools that have driven in-home evaluations and multi-million-dollar revenue impact,
Facilitates scrumban transitions, agile ceremonies, stakeholder communications, and continuous improvement metrics across engineering and business units.

Previously, Harshada held diverse roles across organizations like Deloitte, Cognizant, and Neosoft Technologies, ranging from client-facing product ownership to UI/UX development, showcasing her end-to-end product lifecycle expertise. Her strong foundation in user interface, data analytics, business managementL, and cross-functional communication enables her to make informed product decisions grounded in evidence and user need.

She holds a Master’s in Management Information Systems from the University at Buffalo and is certified in:

SAFe 6.0 Product Owner/Product Manager,
Advanced Certified Scrum Product Owner (A-CSPO),
Certified Scrum Master (CSM),
Lean Six Sigma Black Belt.
She is currently pursuing her PMP certification.

Harshada is a systems thinker and a storyteller, equally comfortable translating business problems into product solutions as she is shaping strategy from raw data. She is passionate about delivering value, reducing friction in the systems, and creating scalable tools that serve both users and organizations.
