Streamlining Data Ingestion for Healthcare Interoperability

Project Overview
Company: Signify Health
Role: Product Owner, Data Ingestion Team
Duration: Jan 2023 - Dec 2023
Product Type: Internal Data Platform
Problem Statement
The pipeline was error-prone and delayed. Goal: Improve ingestion reliability, reduce latency, align with standards.
Discovery & Research
Collaborated with compliance and analytics teams. Audited errors and identified 3 root causes.
Product Strategy
Defined MVP monitoring, prioritized formats, standardized schema contracts.
Execution
Built ingestion pipelines, implemented validation, led agile ceremonies, piloted a new data feed.
Results
Latency: 24 hrs -> 4 hrs Errors: 7% -> 1.5% Reprocessing time reduced by 80%.
Lessons
Early alignment avoids rework. Schema contracts scale ingestion. Visibility builds trust
